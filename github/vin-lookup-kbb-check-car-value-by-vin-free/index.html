Common mistakes buyers make when using free VIN value checks

What to avoid

A common mistake is treating a VIN-based KBB range as a guaranteed sale price; KBB returns ranges because condition, options, and local demand change outcomes, so expect a band rather than a single number Kelley Blue Book value guide

Another error is skipping title checks; assuming no accident or salvage because a quick search showed no results risks missing records that appear only in NMVTIS or in paid reports NMVTIS consumer access information

A reliable habit is to cross-check multiple valuation sources and local listings, and to use price analysis labels and service history to narrow your expectations rather than relying on a single free result FTC guidance on buying a used car
