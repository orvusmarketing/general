How to evaluate a $30k tesla for sale: decision checklist

Title, ownership and service checks come first. Confirm title status, look for single owner versus multiple owners, and review any reported accidents or salvage history. These items directly affect resale value and can point to prior repairs that deserve a closer look Consumer Reports buying a used Tesla.

Battery and range considerations are next. Ask the seller for estimated battery health or recent range tests and compare that against expectations for the model year. Inspections, warranties and total cost to own round out the checklist. Arrange an independent pre-purchase inspection that includes battery and charging system checks.
