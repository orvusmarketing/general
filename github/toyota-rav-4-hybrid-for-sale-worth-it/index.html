Key risks to verify on a used listing

The top VIN-level risks for used hybrid RAV4s include prior accident or flood damage, incomplete hybrid repairs, missing service history, and outstanding recalls or technical service bulletins; these items can materially affect both safety and long-term cost Consumer Reports reliability overview.

Flood or structural damage is especially important for hybrids because water exposure can compromise electrical components and cooling pathways; a clean title and no flood indicators are strong positive signals but confirm with a detailed history report and inspection IIHS safety and crashworthiness resources.
