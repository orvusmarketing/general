Inspection checklist and a concise test-drive script

Before the test drive, request full service records and run a vehicle history report. When inspecting the car, look for signs of suspension repair or leaks, uneven tire wear and any dashboard warning lights. For common inspection priorities, see aggregated repair data RepairPal reliability page.

  • Walkaround: check for fluid leaks, uneven ride height and tire wear
  • Interior and electronics: test infotainment, climate controls and driver assistance features
  • Semi-static checks: verify wheel torque and observe brake feel while cold
  • Document requests: service invoices, title documents, recall repair orders

Test-drive script: start with gentle city driving to check idle behavior and electronics, then a highway run for sustained power delivery. Try firm braking and listen for suspension noises on a varied road surface. End with a final interior check for any new warning lights or strange indicators.
