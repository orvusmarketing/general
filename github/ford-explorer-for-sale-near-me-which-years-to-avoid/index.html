Why certain years show more complaints and recalls

What owner reported databases track

Aggregated owner report sites collect complaints, repair patterns, and recurring problem descriptions from vehicle owners and repair records to reveal clusters by model year; these sources help identify higher complaint counts for 2011 through 2014 and for the 2016 to 2017 range, which commonly involve powertrain and transmission issues in reported cases RepairPal Explorer reliability page and RepairPal problems list

What NHTSA recall and investigation records tell us

NHTSA maintains recall and investigation records that can cover multiple model years for the same defect, so whether a recall appears for one year or several depends on the affected production range and the remedy; checking the NHTSA vehicle page for a specific VIN or model year helps surface official safety recalls and investigations NHTSA vehicle page

How to assess an individual Ford Explorer before you buy

VIN level checks and vehicle history reports

Start every listing by running a VIN level vehicle history report to confirm title status, ownership history, reported accidents, and any recorded service events; a VIN check is the most direct way to see issues that matter more than model year alone NHTSA vehicle page

When aggregated datasets flag a model year cluster, use the VIN to confirm whether this individual vehicle shares the typical problems reported for that year or instead has a clean service and ownership record RepairPal Explorer reliability page

Buyers often avoid 2011 to 2014 and approach 2016 to 2017 with caution due to aggregated complaint clusters; always verify a specific vehicle with a VIN level history report and inspection.

Pre purchase inspection and test drive focus areas

Ask a qualified technician to inspect the transmission for wear, check for engine oil or coolant leaks, look for frame, undercarriage, or flood indicators, confirm suspension condition, and run a road test that checks shifting behavior under load; particular attention to transmission function is important when a year is known for powertrain complaints CarComplaints Explorer page

For safety features, confirm that active driver aids reported on the listing are present and functional for the vehicle trim, since IIHS and other safety ratings vary by trim and specific equipment IIHS Explorer ratings
