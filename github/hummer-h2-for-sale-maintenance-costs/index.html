Parts supply, lead times and shop costs

Why discontinued models can mean pricier or slower parts sourcing

When a model is out of production, dealers and parts suppliers may hold limited stock, and some components become low‑volume items. That can raise parts cost or extend the time before a vehicle is repaired, which increases labor charges and downtime RepairPal Hummer H2 page.

How hourly rates and regional availability affect the repair bill

Labor rates and the presence of independent specialists versus dealer networks affect final repair bills. In areas with few specialists for larger, discontinued SUVs, shops may charge a premium or need to order parts from distant suppliers, stretching times and costs KBB Hummer H2 page.
