Quick answer: what buyers report as the most common problem on the Lexus RX350

Across owner complaint databases and safety records, driveline and transmission behavior is the single area that comes up most often. Reports describe shudder, hesitation, or hard shifts during acceleration and downshifts, and these symptoms are cited frequently enough that they should be the first thing a used buyer checks on a test drive. Suspension wear and adaptive-suspension concerns to check

Recalls and Technical Service Bulletins: what they tell you and how to act

A recall is an official safety action that a manufacturer must remedy, while a TSB is guidance for dealerships about common faults and repair procedures.
