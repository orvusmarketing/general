How to use market-cap math to check a $0.01 claim

The formula: market cap = price × circulating supply

Step 1, the basic formula is market-cap equals price times circulating supply. This arithmetic is how exchanges and trackers report market capitalization and it is the starting point for any claim that a token can reach a given price Market Capitalization (Market Cap) Definition.

Step 3, multiply price by supply to get market-cap. For a price of $0.01 and supply of 5.89×10^14, the implied market-cap is roughly $5.9 trillion.
