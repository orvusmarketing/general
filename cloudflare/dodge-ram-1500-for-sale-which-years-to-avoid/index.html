Why model year matters when buying a Ram 1500

How model updates changed safety and features

The model year of a Ram 1500 can change what safety technology, powertrain options, and structural refinements the truck includes, and those differences can affect both daily ownership and long term repair exposure. For example, later model years added more driver assistance options and structural updates that improved crash avoidance and occupant protection, which may matter to buyers comparing older and newer listings IIHS vehicle ratings.
