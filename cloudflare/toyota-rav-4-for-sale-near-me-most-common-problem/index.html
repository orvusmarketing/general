Hybrid-system faults and inverter issues on early RAV4 hybrids

How hybrid faults typically present

Early hybrid RAV4 models report hybrid inverter and electrical control faults more often than later generations, and these faults can present as warning lights, reduced power mode events or sudden declines in fuel economy and electric assist behavior.
