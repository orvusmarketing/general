The short answer is that the Toyota Highlander is larger overall than the Toyota RAV4, based on manufacturer specifications and independent comparisons. Common mistakes buyers make when comparing size

A frequent error is comparing different model years or trims and assuming numbers transfer directly. Another mistake is misreading cargo figures by not matching the seat configuration. Always compare cargo behind the same number of rows to avoid drawing the wrong conclusion about which vehicle offers more practical rear storage 2024 RAV4 Features & Specifications.

Quick in person checklist to test comfort and cargo

Bring a tape measure and measure third row legroom, second row shoulder room and trunk depth with the seats in the configuration you expect to use most. Recording a few simple numbers in person prevents surprises when you start to load the vehicle at home.

Test seat folding speed and latch mechanisms, check that the LATCH anchors are accessible for child seats, and load a typical item you carry to test floor height and clearance. These are practical checks that confirm whether published cargo capacity is usable for your needs.
