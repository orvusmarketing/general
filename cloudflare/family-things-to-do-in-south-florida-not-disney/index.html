Quick overview: family-friendly options beyond the major theme parks

South Florida has many options for families who want to spend time outdoors, learn at interactive museums or visit zoos without going to large theme parks; these choices include public beaches, state parks, national park experiences, science centers and family-oriented animal facilities, according to Visit Florida guidance Visit Florida.

Expect seasonal weather to shape what you can comfortably do, and allow extra time for travel when you include parks or museums that are further from central transit corridors, a detail regional guidance emphasizes Visit Florida.
