Quick overview: what a used Lexus IS 350 is and why model year matters

The Lexus IS 350 is a compact luxury sedan offered in several generations and trims, powered typically by a V6 and positioned as a sport oriented model in the Lexus lineup. Risk and reliability for a used Lexus IS 350 vary by model year, trim and production run rather than because of a single universal defect. Industry summaries and safety databases show that model year differences change the practical risk buyers face when evaluating a specific VIN, so year level checks are essential for a careful purchase decision. How to interpret recalls and technical service bulletins

Recalls indicate safety related defects that the manufacturer should fix at no cost when the VIN is in scope. Technical service bulletins describe recurring faults and recommended repairs but are not the same as recalls. If a VIN shows many TSB related repairs, check whether the service history records the work and whether the fixes were performed by a dealer or independent shop. NHTSA vehicle safety pages

If you find open recalls or a pattern of complaints for the same system, ask the seller for documentation proving the recall or bulletin has been addressed for that VIN.
