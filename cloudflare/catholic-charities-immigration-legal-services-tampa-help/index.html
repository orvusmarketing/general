Who provides legal advice: attorneys, accredited representatives, and referrals

Clients at Catholic Charities sites may be assisted by staff attorneys, by pro bono private lawyers working through the office, or by supervised non-attorney accredited representatives who are authorized under the Department of Justice program that recognizes organizations and accredits representatives.
